Types of Disasters

Flood

Heavy rainfall may cause floods. Avoid waterlogged areas and move to safer places.

Earthquake

Stay calm during an earthquake. Drop, Cover and Hold until the shaking stops.

Fire

Use emergency exits immediately and never use elevators during a fire.

Cyclone

Stay indoors and keep following weather updates issued by the authorities.

About the System

The Disaster Management System is a simple website that spreads awareness about natural disasters. It helps users learn safety guidelines, check disaster alerts and understand how to stay prepared during emergencies.
