Safety Guidelines

Follow these simple precautions to stay safe before, during and after a disaster.

Flood

Move to higher ground, avoid flooded roads and keep drinking water stored safely.

Earthquake

Drop, Cover and Hold. Stay away from windows and heavy furniture during the shaking.

Fire

Use stairs instead of elevators and leave the building immediately through the nearest exit.

Cyclone

Stay indoors, secure loose objects and follow official weather updates.

Heatwave

Drink plenty of water, wear light clothing and avoid direct sunlight during peak hours.

Landslide

Avoid travelling through hilly areas during heavy rainfall and follow evacuation instructions.
