About Disaster Management System

The Disaster Management System is a simple website created to spread awareness about natural disasters and help people stay prepared during emergencies. It provides information about different types of disasters, safety measures, emergency contacts and disaster alerts.

Our Objectives

Spread Awareness

Help people understand different disasters and the steps they should take to stay safe.

Share Alerts

Provide users with important information and updates during emergency situations.

Safety Guidelines

Make disaster preparedness simple by providing useful precautions and emergency instructions.

Features

Disaster Alerts

View information about floods, earthquakes, cyclones and other disasters.

Incident Reporting

Users can fill out an incident report form to share disaster information.

Emergency Contacts

Quick access to important emergency helpline numbers during critical situations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a simple platform where people can learn about disasters, stay informed and become better prepared for emergency situations.
